Lasagna Recipe

Descripton

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound lean ground beef
  2. 1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  3. 32 ounces cottage cheese
  4. 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  5. 2 eggs
  6. ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  7. 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  8. salt to taste
  9. ground black pepper to taste
  10. 9 lasagna noodles
  11. ½ cup water
