Lasagna Recipe

Descripton

Here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade easy lasagna: Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound lean ground beef
  2. 1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  3. 32 ounces cottage cheese
  4. 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  5. 2 eggs
  6. ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  7. 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  8. salt to taste
  9. ground black pepper to taste
  10. 9 lasagna noodles
  11. ½ cup water
